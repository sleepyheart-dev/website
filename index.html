About

I am a medical graduate and PhD candidate in sleep medicine at Oslo University Hospital and the University of Oslo. My research explores psychological, behavioural and physiological mechanisms linking insomnia, circadian rhythms, and cardiometabolic health.

Publications appear under A P Pacheco (André Pekkola Pacheco).

Research

  • Insomnia & sleep quality — behavioural mechanisms and treatment.
  • Circadian biology — misalignment/variability and cardiometabolic risk.
  • Heart–brain–gut axes — sleep, emotion regulation, systemic health.

Future Directions

I aim to examine how cognitive-behavioural and stress-regulating interventions influence sleep–cardiometabolic pathways, integrating behavioural, physiological, and registry-based data.
